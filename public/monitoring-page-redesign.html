Picking by user goal

"I want to glance and know"

→ V1 Cockpit (default) · or V6 Calendar (weekly).

"I want to dig into performance"

→ V5 Strategy Lab or V8 Notebook.

"I want to feel something"

→ V3 Editorial (narrative) or V9 Game HUD (gamified).

"I want to debug / audit"

→ V4 Mission Control (events) or V2 Terminal.

"I want to talk to the bot"

→ V7 Cypheus Chat. Highest bet on the AI narrative.
